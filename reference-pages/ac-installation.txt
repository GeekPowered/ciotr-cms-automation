## Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I know if I need a new AC system?
A: If your air conditioner is over 12–15 years old, requires frequent repairs, struggles to cool your home, or causes rising energy bills, it may be time to consider replacement. Our technicians can evaluate your system and recommend the most cost-effective solution.

Q: How long does AC installation take?
A: Most residential AC installations can be completed in one day, depending on the system's complexity and ductwork requirements. Our team works efficiently while ensuring proper setup and performance testing.

Q: What size air conditioner do I need?
A: System size depends on your home’s square footage, insulation, airflow, and layout. We perform load calculations to ensure your new unit delivers optimal comfort and efficiency.

Q: Will a new AC system lower my energy bills?
A: Yes. Modern high-efficiency systems use less energy while delivering better cooling performance, thereby significantly reducing monthly utility costs.

Q: Do you install heat pumps and complete HVAC systems?
A: Absolutely. We install air conditioners, heat pumps, thermostats, and full HVAC systems to ensure total home comfort.
