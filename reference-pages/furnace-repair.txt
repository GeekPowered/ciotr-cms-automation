## Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I know if I need furnace repair in Austin?
A: If your furnace produces weak heat, strange sounds, cold spots, or a sudden rise in energy bills, it’s time to schedule a professional inspection.

Q: How long does a furnace typically last?
A: Most furnaces last 15–20 years with regular maintenance. Scheduling annual tune-ups helps extend lifespan and improve efficiency.

Q: Is it better to repair or replace my furnace?
A: A technician will evaluate the cost, age, and condition of your system. Older units with frequent repairs may be better candidates for replacement.

Q: Can furnace issues affect indoor air quality?
A: Yes. Dirty filters, clogged ducts, or malfunctioning components can circulate dust, pollutants, or allergens throughout the home.

Q: Do you work with heat pumps as well?
A: Absolutely. Our team repairs, installs, and maintains heat pumps, furnaces, and complete HVAC systems across Austin.
