## Expert Water Softening Solutions for the Austin Area

At Cold is on the Right, we believe everyone deserves high-quality water in their homes. Our water softener systems use advanced ion exchange processes to remove hardness minerals like calcium and magnesium, replacing them with sodium ions that create soft water. This not only protects your plumbing system but also helps prevent skin irritation, smelly water, and stubborn water spots. We provide customized water treatment equipment designed to give you a better life. Our team installs and maintains whole-house water systems, offering both traditional salt-based solutions and advanced filtration systems to address chlorine, contaminants, and other chemicals present in tap water.

## Signs You Need a Water Softener

Chalky white residue on faucets, fixtures, or showers

Smelly water or unpleasant taste in drinking water

Water spots on dishes, glassware, or laundry

Dry, irritated skin and hair after showers

Appliances or plumbing systems are wearing down from buildup

## Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I know if I need a water softener?
A: If you notice hard water stains, residue on fixtures, or skin irritation after showers, it’s time to consider water softening. A free water test can confirm the level of hardness minerals in your water.

Q: What type of water softener system is best?
A: We install high-quality water softener systems designed for Austin’s city water, as well as homes in areas like Dripping Springs and Round Rock. Options include ion exchange systems and whole-house water filtration.

Q: How much does a water softener cost?
A: The cost depends on the size of your home, water conditions, and the type of system you choose. We provide estimates and consultations before any installation.

Q: Does a water softener require maintenance?
A: Yes. Regularly adding salt to the brine solution tank and scheduling routine inspections keep your system running efficiently and extend its lifespan.
